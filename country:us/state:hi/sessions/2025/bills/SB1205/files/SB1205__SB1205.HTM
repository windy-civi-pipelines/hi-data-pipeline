NO. 1205 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT Relating to State Organization and Administration. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 26, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§26-    Reorganization; report.  Any department, office, or other state agency shall, upon making a final determination to reorganize, submit a report to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the immediately following regular session.  The report shall include: (1)  An estimated timeline for the completion of the reorganization; and (2)  The divisions and positions affected by the reorganization." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: State Departments, Offices, and Agencies; Reorganization; Report Description: Requires departments, offices, and other state agencies that make a final determination to reorganize to submit a report to the Legislature.  Requires the report to include an estimated timeline for the completion of the reorganization and the divisions and positions affected by the reorganization.
