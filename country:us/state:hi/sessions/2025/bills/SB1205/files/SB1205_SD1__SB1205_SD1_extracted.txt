Section 1:
SB1205 SD1 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 1205 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO STATE ORGANIZATION AND ADMINISTRATION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 26, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§26-    Policy and procedures for effecting organizational and functional statement changes; report.  (a)  Any state executive branch department, office, or other agency shall comply with directive number 19-02, Hawaii Administrative Directives, as extended by directive number 23-01, Hawaii Administrative Directives, when making changes in its organization or functions. (b)  Each state executive branch department, office, or other agency shall submit a report of its compliance pursuant to subsection (a) to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the regular session of 2026 and every year thereafter." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Report Title: Executive Branch; State Departments, Offices, and Agencies; Organization; Reports Description: Requires state executive branch departments, offices, and other agencies to comply with directive number 19-02, Hawaii Administrative Directives, as extended by directive number 23‑01, Hawaii Administrative Directives.  Requires reports to the Legislature.  (SD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
