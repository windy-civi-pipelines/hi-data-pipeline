NO. 940 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII AT HILO. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $ or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the rat lungworm lab, also known as the Jarvi Lab, at the Daniel K. Inouye college of pharmacy at the university of Hawaii at Hilo. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the university of Hawaii for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: UH Hilo; Rat Lungworm Lab; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds for the rat lungworm lab at the University of Hawaii at Hilo.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent. ================================================================================ Raw Text: HB940 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B.
