Aloha President Kouchi and Speaker Nakamura:
The Hawai'i Government Employees Association and the employer have reached a
tentative agreement concerning collective bargaining for Unit 2 (Blue Collar
Supervisors). Notice of ratification of the agreement was received on April 24, 2025. In
accordance with Section 89-11, HRS, cost items must be submitted to the Legislature
for appropriation. The following is a summary of the included and excluded
requirements of the settlement for the current fiscal biennium which are being
transmitted to the Legislature for appropriation:
FY 26 FY 27 Biennium
Salaries & Fringe
General $1,075,938 $2,364,077 $3,440,015
Special 391,593 861,224 1,252,817
Federal 15,363 33,789 49,152
Revolving 28,636 63,334 91,970
Total $1,511,530 $3,322,424 $4,833,954
In addition, Employer-Union Trust Fund contribution amounts are as follows:
FY 26 FY 27 Biennium
General $319,294 $690,671 $1,009,965
- 2 -
Estimated costs for the third and fourth years of the agreement are also provided for
your reference:
FY28 FY 29 Biennium
Salaries & Fringe
General $3,759,695 $5,233,760 $ 8,993,455
Special 1,370,980 1,908,501 3,279,481
Federal 53,789 74,877 128,666
Revolving 101,408 141,168 242,576
Total $5,285,872 $7,358,306 $12,644,178
Attached is a proposed draft House Bill No. 1028, H.D. 1, S.D. 1, G.D. 1, which has
been updated to reflect estimated collective bargaining amounts.
Mahala,
