A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO VETERANS CEMETERIES.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The purpose of this Act is to establish the
2 veterans cemeteries board, composed of representatives from
3 federal, state, and county departments and agencies, to provide
4 veterans cemeteries in the State with guidance, education,
5 technical assistance, and other support to ensure compliance
6 with standards set by the National Cemetery Administration of
7 the United States Department of Veterans Affairs.
8 SECTION 2. Chapter 363, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
9 amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated
10 and to read as follows:
11 '' §363- Veterans cemeteries board. (a) There shall be
12 established the veterans cemeteries board within the office in
13 the department of defense for administrative purposes only. The
14 veterans cemeteries board shall:
15 (1) Identify compliance issues faced by veterans
16 cemeteries in the State, the resources needed, and the
17 challenges encountered by departments or agencies that
