C.D. 1
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. There is appropriated out of the general
2 revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $215,552 or so much
3 thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the
4 same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year
5 2026-2027 for the university of Hawaii, West Oahu (UOH 700) for
6 the pre-nursing pathway program, including for the establishment
7 of two full-time equivalent (2.0) positions, to be allocated as
8 follows:
9 (1) $107,776 for one full-time equivalent (1.0 FTE)
10 program coordinator; and
11 (2) $107,776 for one full-time equivalent (1.0 FTE)
12 academic advisor coordinator.
13 The sums appropriated shall be expended by the university
14 of Hawaii for the purposes of this Act.
15 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
