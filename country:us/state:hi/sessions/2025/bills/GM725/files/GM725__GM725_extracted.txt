KE KE'ENA O KE KIA'AINA
JOSH GREEN, M.D.
GOVERNOR
KE KIA'AINA
March 11,2025
The Honorable Ronald D. Kouchi, President
and Members of the Senate
Thirty-Third Legislature
Hawai'i State Capitol, Room 409
Honolulu, HI 96813
Aloha President Kouchi and Members of the Senate:
have the honor to submit to the Senate for advise and consent the nomination of Scott K.D.
Shishido to serve as a Circuit Court Judge in the Circuit Court of the Third Circuit (island of
Hawai'i), State of Hawai'i, for a term of ten years.
Mahalo,
osh Green, M.D.
Governor, State of Hawai'i
C: The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura, Speaker, State House of Representatives
The Honorable Karl Rhoads, Chairperson, Senate Committee on Judiciary
The Honorable Mark E. Recktenwald, Chief Justice, Hawai'i Supreme Court
The Honorable Wendy M. DeWeese, Chief Judge, Third Circuit Court

EXECUTIVE CHAMBERS
KE KE'ENA O KE KIA'AINA
JOSH GREEN, M.D.
GOVERNOR
KE KIA'AINA
March 11,2025
The Honorable Ronald D. Kouchi, President
and Members of the Senate
Thirty-Third Legislature
Hawai'i State Capitol, Room 409
Honolulu, HI 96813
Aloha President Kouchi and Members of the Senate:
In accordance with the provisions of Article VI, Section 3 of the Hawai'i State Constitution, I
have the honor to submit to the Senate for advise and consent the nomination of Scott K.D.
Shishido to serve as a Circuit Court Judge in the Circuit Court of the Third Circuit (island of
Hawai'i), State of Hawai'i, for a term of ten years.
Mahalo,
osh Green, M.D.
Governor, State of Hawai'i
C: The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura, Speaker, State House of Representatives
The Honorable Karl Rhoads, Chairperson, Senate Committee on Judiciary
The Honorable Mark E. Recktenwald, Chief Justice, Hawai'i Supreme Court
The Honorable Wendy M. DeWeese, Chief Judge, Third Circuit Court
