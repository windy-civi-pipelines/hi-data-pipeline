Section 1:
SCR35 THE SENATE S.C.R. NO. 35 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE HAWAII TECHNOLOGY DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION to INITIATE the planning of a health care Technology Hub in kakaako to accelerate the adoption of health care technology and promote the development and growth of Hawaii's highly skilled health care workforce. WHEREAS, the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation is a dynamic state agency responsible for diversifying Hawaii's economy through the development of a flourishing technology industry; and WHEREAS, technology is transforming health care in many ways, including improving patient care, diagnostics, and treatment; and WHEREAS, examples of technological advancements that will transform health care include virtual reality and augmented reality for training simulations and patient treatment and generative artificial intelligence to simplify diagnostics and surgery; and WHEREAS, the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation's Entrepreneurs Sandbox is located at 643 Ilalo Street, Honolulu, with vacant land surrounding the building; and WHEREAS, the Entrepreneurs Sandbox is located adjacent to the University of Hawaii at Manoa John A. Burns School of Medicine and the University of Hawaii Cancer Center; and WHEREAS, because the Entrepreneurs Sandbox is located in Kakaako Makai within the Kakaako Community Development District, any development in that area must be approved by the Hawaii Community Development Authority; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, the House of Representatives concurring, that the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation is requested to initiate the planning of a Health Care Technology Hub in Kakaako to accelerate the adoption of health care technology and promote the development and growth of Hawaii's highly skilled health care workforce; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation is requested to initiate the planning of the Health Care Technology Hub in consultation with the University of Hawaii at Manoa John A. Burns School of Medicine, University of Hawaii Cancer Center, Hawaii Community Development Authority, and Hawaii Broadband and Digital Equity Office; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation is requested to submit a report of its conceptual plan for the development of the Health Care Technology Hub, including any proposed legislation, to the Legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the Regular Session of 2026; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Concurrent Resolution be transmitted to the Executive Director of the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation, Dean of the University of Hawaii at Manoa John A. Burns School of Medicine, Director of the University of Hawaii Cancer Center, Executive Director of the Hawaii Community Development Authority, and Strategic Broadband Coordinator of the Hawaii Broadband and Digital Equity Office. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Health Care Technology Hub; Health Care Technology; HTDC; JABSOM; UH Cancer Center; HCDA; Hawaii Broadband and Digital Equity Office
