A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO THE HAWAII ABLE SAVINGS PROGRAM.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The purpose of this Act is to:
2 (1) Repeal statutory language that gives the director of
3 finance discretion to use moneys in the Hawaii ABLE
4 savings program trust fund if the director elects to
5 accept deposits from contributors instead of sending
6 deposits directly to the ABLE program manager;
7 (2) Authorize the director of finance to expend moneys in
8 the Hawaii ABLE savings program trust fund to provide
9 incentive payments to ABLE account owners;
10 (3) Make an appropriation to the department of budget and
11 finance to fund incentive payments to ABLE account
12 owners; and
13 (4) Make an appropriation to the department of health to
14 establish one full-time equivalent (1.0 FTE) permanent
15 position to provide statewide outreach, advocacy, and
16 relationship management for the Hawaii ABLE savings
17 program.
