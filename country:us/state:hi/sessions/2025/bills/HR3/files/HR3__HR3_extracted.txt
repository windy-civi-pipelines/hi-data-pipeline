HR3 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 3 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION ELECTING THE OFFICERS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE THIRTY-third LEGISLATURE. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that BRIAN L. TAKESHITA, ROD TANONAKA, RUPERT JUAREZ, and JADE VILLANUEVA be elected as Chief Clerk, Sergeant-at-Arms, Assistant Chief Clerk, and Assistant Sergeant-at-Arms, respectively, of the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii.
