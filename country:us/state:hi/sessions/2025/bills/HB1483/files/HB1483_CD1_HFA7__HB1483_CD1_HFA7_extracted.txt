Section 1:
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AMENDMENT TO: H.B. No. 1483, H.D. 1, S.D. 1, C.D. 1 OFFERED BY: Representative David A. Tarnas DATE: SECTION 1.  House Bill No. 1483, H.D. 1, S.D. 1, C.D. 1, is amended by amending subsection (a) of section 132D-B on p. 7, lines 13-20, to read as follows: "(a)  A person commits the offense of sending or receiving fireworks or articles pyrotechnic by air delivery if the person intentionally, knowingly, or recklessly sends or receives any amount of aerial devices, articles pyrotechnic, consumer fireworks, or display fireworks via any form of air delivery, including but not limited to any private courier, commercial carrier, or mail or postal services; provided that a person possessing a current explosives license issued by the federal Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives shall not be prosecuted under this section if the person sends or receives the consumer fireworks, aerial devices, display fireworks, or articles pyrotechnic via any form of air delivery in compliance with federal law." CARRIED FAILED TO CARRY WITHDRAWN CHIEF CLERK, HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
