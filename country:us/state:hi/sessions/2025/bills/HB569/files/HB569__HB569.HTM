NO. 569 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to education. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 302A, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§302A-    Community policing programs.  (a)  The department shall collaborate with the department of law enforcement and respective county police departments to offer the following programs in all department schools: (1)  Drug Abuse Resistance Education (D.A.R.E.); (2)  Law Enforcement Explorers Programs (L.E.E.P.); and (3)  Police Activities Leagues (P.A.L.). (b)  There shall be a designated school faculty member liaison to administer the programs listed in paragraph (a).  For each school with a school resource officer, the officer shall serve as the designated school faculty member liaison. (c)  The department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 to effectuate the purposes of this section." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Community Policing; Education; Programs; PAL; DARE; LEEP Description: Requires all department schools to offer community policing programs including DARE, LEEP, and PAL.  Authorizes the department of education to work with the department of law enforcement and local police departments to implement the programs. Requires a faculty liaison.
