HB96 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 96 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to BIOSECURITY. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The purpose of this Act is to effectuate the title of this Act.
