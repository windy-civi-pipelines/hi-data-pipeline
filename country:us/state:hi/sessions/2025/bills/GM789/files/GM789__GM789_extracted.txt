EXECUTIVE CHAMBERS
KE KE'ENA O KE KIA'AINA
JOSH GREEN, M.D.
GOVERNOR
KE KIA'AINA
April 2, 2025
The Honorable Ronald D. Kouchi, President
and Members of the Senate
Thirty-Third Legislature
Hawaii State Capitol, Room 409
Honolulu, Hawaii 96813
Dear President Kouchi and Members of the Senate:
Constitution, I have the honor to submit to the Senate for advice and consent the
nomination of Taryn R. Tomasa Gifford to serve as a Circuit Court Judge in the Circuit
Court of the First Circuit (island of O'ahu), State of Hawai'i, for a term of ten years.
Sincerely,
Governor, State of Hawaii
c: The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura, Speaker, State House of Representatives
The Honorable Karl Rhoads, Chairperson, Senate Committee on Judiciary
The Honorable Mark E. Recktenwald, Chief Justice, Hawai'i State Supreme Court
The Honorable Jeanette Castagnetti, Chief Judge, First Circuit Court

Raw Text:
EXECUTIVE CHAMBERS
KE KE'ENA O KE KIA'AINA
JOSH GREEN, M.D.
GOVERNOR
KE KIA'AINA
April 2, 2025
The Honorable Ronald D. Kouchi, President
and Members of the Senate
Thirty-Third Legislature
Hawaii State Capitol, Room 409
Honolulu, Hawaii 96813
Dear President Kouchi and Members of the Senate:
In accordance with the provisions of Article VI, Section 3 of the Hawaii State
Constitution, I have the honor to submit to the Senate for advice and consent the
nomination of Taryn R. Tomasa Gifford to serve as a Circuit Court Judge in the Circuit
Court of the First Circuit (island of O'ahu), State of Hawai'i, for a term of ten years.
Sincerely,
Governor, State of Hawaii
c: The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura, Speaker, State House of Representatives
The Honorable Karl Rhoads, Chairperson, Senate Committee on Judiciary
The Honorable Mark E. Recktenwald, Chief Justice, Hawai'i State Supreme Court
The Honorable Jeanette Castagnetti, Chief Judge, First Circuit Court
