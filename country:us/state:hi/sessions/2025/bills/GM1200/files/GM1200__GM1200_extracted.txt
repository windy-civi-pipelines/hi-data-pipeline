C.D. 1
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO BEACH MANAGEMENT ON THE NORTH SHORE OF OAHU.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII;
1 SECTION I. Act 226, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, is
2 amended as follows:
3 1. By amending subsection (d) of section 2 to read:
4 "(d) The university of Hawaii sea grant college program
5 shall submit the north shore beach management plan to the
6 governor and legislature by December 1, [2-U25-r] 2026."
7 2. By amending section 3 to read:
8 "SECTION 3. There is appropriated out of the general
9 revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $1,000,000 or so much
10 thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2024-2025 for the
11 university of Hawaii sea grant college program to develop a
12 north shore beach management plan for the area from Sunset point
13 to Kapo‘o (Sharks Cove) pursuant to section 2 of this Act[T]/_
14 provided that the appropriation made by this Act shall not lapse
15 at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is
16 made; provided further that all moneys from the appropriation
17 unencumbered as of June 30, 2026, shall lapse as of that date.
