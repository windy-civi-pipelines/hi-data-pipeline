NO. 690 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to agriculture. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: PART I SECTION 1. The legislature finds that reliable agricultural infrastructure that supports the State's land and water resources is the lifeline of Hawaii's agriculture industry.  Increasing drought events, rain bombs, wildfires, and other crises have highlighted the need for the timely repair and maintenance of the State's agricultural infrastructure.  Sufficient funding is necessary to maintain and improve the State's irrigation systems to ensure that Hawaii's crops receive a steady and dependable supply of water.  Additionally, funding to develop the infrastructure of agricultural lands would provide not only access and other support for local producers to become financially viable, but also new and innovative opportunities to grow the agriculture industry in the State. Accordingly, the purpose of this part is to appropriate funds for capital improvement projects for the agricultural infrastructure of the State. SECTION 2. The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $342,070,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof The appropriation made for the capital improvement project authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2028, shall lapse as of that date. PART II SECTION 4. There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $9,500,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the establishment, operation, and administration of a food and product innovation network. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the agribusiness development corporation for the purposes of this part. SECTION 5. There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $4,411,426 or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the retirement of outstanding debt on the Waiahole water system. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the agribusiness development corporation for the purposes of this Act. PART III SECTION 6. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
