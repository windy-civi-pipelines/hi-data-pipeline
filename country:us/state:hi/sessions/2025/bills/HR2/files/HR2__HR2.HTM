NO. 2 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION ELECTING THE SPEAKER AND VICE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE THIRTY-third LEGISLATURE. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that the Honorable NADINE K. NAKAMURA, Representative from the FIFTEENTH DISTRICT, and the Honorable LINDA ICHIYAMA, Representative from the THIRTY-FIRST DISTRICT, be elected as Speaker and Vice Speaker, respectively, of the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii.
