NO. 217 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII SENATE RESOLUTION INFORMING the HOUSE AND GOVERNOR THAT THE SENATE IS READY TO ADJOURN SINE DIE. BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-Third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that the Senate stands ready to adjourn Sine Die; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the House of Representatives and the Governor forthwith.
