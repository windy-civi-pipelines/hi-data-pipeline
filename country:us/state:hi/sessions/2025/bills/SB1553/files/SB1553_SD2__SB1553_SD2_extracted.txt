Section 1:
SB1553 SD2 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 1553 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HAWAIIAN HOME LANDS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The purpose of this Act is to appropriate funds to the department of Hawaiian home lands for the purchase of a parcel of land identified as tax map key:  (1) 9-1-013-025, including any existing improvements thereon. SECTION 2. There is appropriated out of the Act 279 special fund established by Act 125, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the purchase of a parcel of land identified as tax map key: (1) 9‑1-013-025, including any existing improvements thereon. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the department of Hawaiian home lands for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2050. Report Title: DHHL; Land Purchase; Act 279 Special Fund; Appropriations Description: Appropriates moneys from the Act 279 Special Fund for the Department of Hawaiian Home Lands to purchase a parcel of land identified as tax map key:  (1) 9-1-013-025.  Effective 7/1/2050.  (SD2) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
