Section 1:
HB228 SD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 228 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII S.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO LICENSE PLATES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 286, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding two new sections to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§286-    License plate flipping devices; prohibited.  (a)  No person shall operate a motor vehicle with, possess, purchase, install, sell, offer for sale, or otherwise distribute a license plate flipping device. (b) Notwithstanding any other provisions of this chapter, any person who violates this section shall be fined no less than $750 but no more than $1,000 for each separate violation. (c) For the purpose of this section "license plate flipping device" means a manual, electric, or mechanical device designed or adapted to be installed on a motor vehicle that: (1)  Switches between two or more license plates for the purpose of allowing a motor vehicle operator to change the license plate displayed on the operator's motor vehicle; or (2)  Hides a license plate from view by flipping the license plate so that the plate number is not visible. §286-    Obscuring of a license plate; prohibited.  Obscuring a vehicle's license plate through the use of a cover or shield shall be prohibited.  Any violation of this section shall be punishable by a fine of no less than $500 but no more than $1,000 for each offense." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: License Plates; Electronic License Plates; License Plate Flipping Devices; Prohibition; Fines Description: Prohibits the use of license plate flipping devices. Establishes minimum and maximum fines. Effective 7/1/3000.  (SD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
