HB1281 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1281 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to capital improvement projects for the benefit of THE Thirty-sixth REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $46,524,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2025-2026 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the thirty-sixth representative district, as follows: 1.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for 1st Circle covered walkway Construction                 $1,500,000 Total funding                     $1,500,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 2.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for administrative building office layout Construction                   $750,000 Total funding                       $750,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 3.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for canopy to existing play areas Construction                   $300,000 Total funding                       $300,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 4.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for I Cafe and J Cafe, including reflooring and air conditioning Construction                   $500,000 Total funding                       $500,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 5.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for main cafeteria, including expansion, reflooring, and air conditioning Construction                 $7,000,000 Total funding                     $7,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 6.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for new playcourt, including covering Construction                 $5,000,000 Total funding                     $5,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 7.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for phase 2 of new building Construction                $15,000,000 Total funding                    $15,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 8.   August Ahrens elementary school construction for play areas and playground Construction                   $300,000 Total funding                       $300,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 9.   August Ahrens elementary school equipment and appurtenances for library outdoor learning Equipment                    $6,000,000 Total funding                     $6,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 10.  August Ahrens elementary school equipment and appurtenances for library small parking lot Equipment                    $2,000,000 Total funding                     $2,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 11.  August Ahrens elementary school equipment and appurtenances for Tucker Field running track Equipment                      $200,000 Total funding                       $200,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 12.  Waipahu elementary school construction for parking Construction                 $5,000,000 Total funding                     $5,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 13.  Waipahu intermediate school construction for cafeteria bathroom reconfiguration Construction                   $800,000 Total funding                       $800,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 14.  Waipahu intermediate school construction for cafeteria heat abatement Equipment                       $50,000 Total funding                        $50,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 15.  Waipahu intermediate school construction for covered courts with lighting Construction                 $2,000,000 Total funding                     $2,000,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 16.  Waipahu intermediate school construction for general repairs for water main Construction                   $100,000 Total funding                       $100,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. 17.  Waipahu intermediate school equipment and appurtenances for classroom air conditioning Equipment                       $24,000 Total funding                        $24,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2028, shall lapse as of that date. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
