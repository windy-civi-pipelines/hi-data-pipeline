Section 1:
HB840 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 840 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII ATHLETICS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $8,519,341 or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the program and operations of the athletics department at the university of Hawaii, to be allocated as follows: (1)  $3,200,000 for each fiscal year to the university of Hawaii at Manoa athletics department; (2)  $800,000 for each fiscal year to make the university of Hawaii at Hilo athletics department; and (3)  $4,519,341 for each fiscal year to establish forty‑three temporary full-time equivalent (43.0 FTE) positions at the university of Hawaii at Manoa athletics department and provide additional support. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the university of Hawaii for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
