NO. 684 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the board of education. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The board of education shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91, Hawaii Revised Statutes, to prohibit the usage of cell phones in department of education schools. SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: BOE; DOE; Cell Phones; Prohibited; Rules Description: Requires the Board of Education to adopt administrative rules to ban the usage of cell phones at Department of Education schools.
