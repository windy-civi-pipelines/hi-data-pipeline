Section 1:
HB449 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 449 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The legislature finds that the INNOVATE Hawaii program has been a federal program of the Hawaii technology development corporation for over twenty years and is funded by the National Institute of Standards and Technology  manufacturing extension program, with a mission of supporting the reshoring of manufacturing United States federal initiatives while providing funding and technology to each local manufacturing extension program center in each state. INNOVATE Hawaii is part of a network of fifty-two manufacturing extension program centers supporting local manufacturers in each state.  The legislature further finds that funding for INNOVATE Hawaii would be used to bring new, advanced technology to the State to assist small businesses with development, manufacturing, operations, and marketing of "Made in Hawaii" products. Therefore, the purpose of this Act is to promote economic development by appropriating funds for assisting in the development and promotion of Hawaii-manufactured products through the INNOVATE Hawaii program. SECTION 2.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $ or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for assisting in the development and promotion of Hawaii-manufactured products through the INNOVATE Hawaii program. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the Hawaii technology development corporation for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: Economic Development; Hawaii-manufactured Products; Hawaii Technology Development Corporation; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds to assist in the development and promotion of Hawaii-manufactured products through the INNOVATE Hawaii program.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
