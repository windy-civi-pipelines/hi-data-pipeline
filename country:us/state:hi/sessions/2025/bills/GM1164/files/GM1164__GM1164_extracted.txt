A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO LOBBYIST CONTRIBUTIONS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. This Act clarifies administrative oversight of
2 statutory restrictions relating to prohibited lobbyist
3 contributions enacted in Act 128, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023
4 (Act 128). Act 128 left concurrent administrative oversight to
5 the campaign spending and state ethics commissions.
6 For the purpose of administrative efficiency and
7 streamlining of function, this Act clarifies that with respect
8 to the statutory provisions created and amended by Act 128, the
9 campaign spending commission shall have oversight of elected
10 officials, candidates, candidate committees, and others required
11 to file an organizational report with the campaign spending
12 commission, and the ethics board or commission having
13 jurisdiction over the lobbyist shall have oversight of
14 lobbyists.
15 This Act further clarifies that the regular or special
16 sessions in which lobbyist contributions are prohibited are
