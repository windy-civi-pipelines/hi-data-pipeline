Section 1:
HB703 SD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 703 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII S.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO KUPUNA HOUSING. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The legislature finds that the State's kupuna are at significant risk of homelessness.  As rental prices have experienced dramatic increases statewide, more kupuna, who are on fixed incomes, struggle to secure affordable housing. Presently, the state rent supplement program for kupuna provides support for qualified kupuna who are homeless or at imminent risk of being homeless.  However, this critical program will sunset in 2026, creating uncertainty for existing participants in the program.  Therefore, extending the sunset date will ensure continued access to critical rent supplement assistance. Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to extend the sunset date of the state rent supplement program for kupuna to continue to accommodate the housing needs of qualified participants. SECTION 2. Act 98, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023, is amended by amending section 6 to read as follows: "SECTION 6.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2023, and shall be repealed on June 30, [2026.] 2028." SECTION 3. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 4. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2050. Report Title: Kupuna Caucus; State Rent Supplement Program for Kupuna; Kupuna Housing; Sunset; Extension Description: Extends the sunset date for the State Rent Supplement Program for Kupuna to 6/30/2028.  Takes effect 7/1/2050.  (SD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
