HCR184 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.C.R. NO. 184 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION Authorizing HawaiI Community Development Authority to CONDUCT A STUDY TO CONSIDER residential, hotel, and other land uses for THE kakaako makai AREA. WHEREAS, the Hawaii Community Development Authority (HCDA) intends to update the Kakaako Community Development District Makai Area master plan and administrative rules in 2025: and WHEREAS, the planning process will include community and stakeholder engagement, infrastructure assessment, traffic impact analysis, soil contamination assessment, sea level rise analysis, and cultural and historical analysis; and WHEREAS, although the Makai area plan was updated in 2023, it did not contemplate residential, hotel and other uses, nor did it consider soil and geotechnical analysis, cultural and historical analysis, view plan impact of potentially higher densities, and sea level rise on building design and siting; and WHEREAS, in updating the Makai Area Master Plan, the HCDA should include a robust community and stakeholder engagement process, infrastructure assessment, traffic impact analysis, soil contamination assessment, sea level rise analysis, and cultural and historical analysis, among other planning consideration; and WHEREAS, the State continues to suffer from a shortage of affordable housing with fewer families able to afford to buy or rent a home in the State, while Native Hawaiians suffer disproportionately from this lack of affordable housing; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, the Senate concurring, that the HCDA, in conjunction with the updating of the Kakaako Community Development District Makai Area Master Plan, is authorized to: (1)  Consult with the Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation, Office of Hawaiian Affairs, Office of Hawaiian Affairs, Department of Planning and Permitting of the City and County of Honolulu, University of Hawaii at Manoa John A. Burns School of Medicine, Department of Transportation, Department of Business Economic Development and Tourism, Department of Education, and The Kakaako Makai Community Planning Advisory Council; and (2)  Conduct a study to consider residential, hotel, and other land uses in the Kakaako Makai Area which shall include a public hearing before submitting the study to the Legislature. Notice of the public hearing shall be published at least thirty days prior to the hearing and shall include: (a) the date, time, and place of the hearing; (b) a statement of the topic of the hearing; and (c) a description of where, when, and how the study may be viewed by the public. All interested persons may submit data or opinions, orally or in writing, in conjunction with the hearing. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the HCDA shall submit its study considering residential, hotel, and other land uses for the Kakaako Makai Area to the Legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the Regular Session of 2026; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Concurrent Resolution be transmitted to the Executive Director of the Hawaii Community Development Authority, Executive Director of the Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation, Chair of the Board of Trustees of the Office of Hawaiian Affairs, Director of the Department of Planning and Permitting of the City and County of Honolulu, Dean of the University of Hawaii at Manoa John A. Burns School of Medicine, Director of the Department of Transportation, Director of the Department of Business Economic Development and Tourism, Superintendent the Department of Education, and the Chair of The Kakaako Makai Community Planning Advisory Council. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: HCDA; Study to consider residential, hotel, and other land uses for the Kakaako Makai area.
