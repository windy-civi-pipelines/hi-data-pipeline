Section 1:
HR6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 6 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE RULES OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE THIRTy-third LEGISLATURE, INCLUDING THE rules FOR THE SELECT COMMITTEE ON STANDARDS OF CONDUCT. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that the Rules of the House of Representatives in the form attached hereto and made a part hereof be and are hereby adopted; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Rules for the Select Committee on Standards of Conduct established under Rule 62.7 in the form attached hereto and made a part hereof be and are hereby adopted. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Adopting Rules; House of Representatives
