Section 1:
2025-1431 SB938 SD1 SMA. THE SENATE S.B. NO. 938 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII Proposed A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO TAXATION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: Section 1.  The purpose of this Act is to require that the department of taxation submit an annual report to the legislature to ensure organizational stability and prevent overstaffing within the information technology services offices. SECTION 2.  Chapter 231, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§231-    Information technology services office; reports.  (a)  The department shall submit a report to the legislature of its findings and recommendations, including any proposed legislation, no later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session.  The report shall include: (1)  An organizational chart and staffing plan for the information technology services office; and (2)  Specific requirements for: (A)  Cost considerations; (B)  Budget impact; (C)  Management and oversight; (D)  Recruitment and talent acquisition; (E)  Organizational considerations; (F)  Risk of overstaffing; (G)  Long-term sustainability; (H)  Integration of non-technology services staff; and (I)  Accountability measures for effective use of resources and long-term sustainability." SECTION 3.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Report Title: DOTAX; Information Technology Services; Staffing; Reports Description: Requires the Department of Taxation to submit an annual report to the Legislature detailing an organizational chart and staffing plan and requirements for the Information Technology Services Office.  (Proposed SD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
