Section 1:
HB1287 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1287 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to capital improvement projects for the benefit of THE Thirty-fourth REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $650,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2025-2026 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the thirty-fourth representative district, as follows: 1.   Pearl City high school Design and construction for Pearl City high school tennis courts. Design                          $65,000 Construction                   $585,000 Total funding                       $650,000 The sum appropriated for this capital improvement project shall be expended by the department of education. SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2028, shall lapse as of that date. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: CIP; Thirty-fourth Representative District; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds for capital improvement projects in the thirty-fourth representative district.
