Section 1:
HB603 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 603 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO NATIVE HAWAIIAN BUSINESS MARKETING. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $ or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the office of Hawaiian affairs to convene a working group to engage with relevant stakeholders about program design, implementation, and long-term viability of a Native Hawaiian marketing program to promote and support the success of businesses owned by Hawaiians through marketing and technical assistance. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the office of Hawaiian affairs for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2.  The office of Hawaiian affairs shall submit a report to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the regular sessions of 2026 and 2027 on the status of the Native Hawaiian marketing program.  The report shall include: (1)  Information about the program design, implementation, and long-term viability of the Native Hawaiian marketing program; (2)  Any other relevant data; and (3)  Any proposed legislation. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: OHA; Native Hawaiian Marketing Program; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds for the Office of Hawaiian Affairs to convene a working group regarding program design, implementation, and long-term viability of a Native Hawaiian Marketing Program to promote and support the success of businesses owned by Hawaiians through marketing and technical assistance. Effective 7/1/3000. The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
