Section 1:
HB3 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 3 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to beach management on the north shore of oahu. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Act 226, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, section 2, is amended by amending subsection (d) to read as follows: "(d)  The university of Hawaii sea grant college program shall submit the north shore beach management plan to the governor and legislature by December 1, [2025.] 2026." SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
