Section 1:
HB3 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 3 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO BEACH MANAGEMENT ON THE NORTH SHORE OF OAHU. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Act 226, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, section 2, is amended by amending subsection (d) to read as follows: "(d)  The university of Hawaii sea grant college program shall submit the north shore beach management plan to the governor and legislature by December 1, [2025.] 2026." SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: North Shore Beach Management Plan; University of Hawaii; Deadline; Extension Description: Extends the deadline for the University of Hawaii Sea Grant College Program to submit the North Shore Beach Management Plan to the Governor and Legislature.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
