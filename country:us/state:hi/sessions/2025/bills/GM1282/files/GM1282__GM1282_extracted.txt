A BILL FOR AN ACT

1 SECTION 1. There is appropriated out of the general
2 revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $10,000,000 or so
3 much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and
4 the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal
5 year 2026-2027 for the Hawaii public housing authority to
6 rehabilitate, remodel, renovate, and repair housing units.
7 The sums appropriated shall be expended by the Hawaii
8 public housing authority for the purposes of this Act.
9 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
