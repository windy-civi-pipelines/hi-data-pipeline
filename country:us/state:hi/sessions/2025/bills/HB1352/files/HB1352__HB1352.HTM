NO. 1352 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT Making an Appropriation to the University of Hawaii. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $179,378 or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the university of Hawaii i hoa na moolelo program. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the university of Hawaii for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
