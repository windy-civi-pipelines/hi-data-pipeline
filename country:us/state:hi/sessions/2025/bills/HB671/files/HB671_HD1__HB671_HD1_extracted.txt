Section 1:
HB671 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 671 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO NATIVE HAWAIIANS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 10, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§10-     Native Hawaiian notable districts; designation.  (a)  The office shall identify all legislative districts in the State in which at least twenty-five per cent of the population consists of Native Hawaiians. (b) Any district identified by the office pursuant to subsection (a) shall be designated as a Native Hawaiian notable district for purposes of representation and recognition." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: OHA; Native Hawaiians; Legislative Districts; Recognition Description: Requires the Office of Hawaiian Affairs to identify all legislative districts in the State in which at least twenty-five per cent of the population consists of Native Hawaiians.  Designates these districts as Native Hawaiian notable districts. Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
