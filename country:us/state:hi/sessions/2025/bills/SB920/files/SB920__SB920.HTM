NO. 920 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT PROPOSING AN AMENDMENT TO THE HAWAII CONSTITUTION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: Section 1.  The purpose of this Act is to effectuate the title of this Act.
