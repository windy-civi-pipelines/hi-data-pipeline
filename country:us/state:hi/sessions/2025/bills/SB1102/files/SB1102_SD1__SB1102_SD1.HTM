NO. 1102 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE AIRCRAFT RESCUE FIRE FIGHTING UNIT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 261, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§261-    State aircraft rescue fire fighting unit; chief; term limits.  Each appointed chief of the aircraft rescue fire fighting unit of the airports division of the department of transportation shall serve a term of four years.  No chief shall be appointed to more than three terms.  The director of transportation shall appoint the chief from a list of three qualified candidates submitted for consideration." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2050. Report Title: DOT; ARFF; Airports Division; Term Limits Description: Establishes that the Chief of the State Aircraft Rescue Fire Fighting Unit of the Airports Division of the Department of Transportation shall serve not more than three terms of four years each.  Requires the Director of Transportation to appoint the Chief from a list of three qualified candidates submitted for consideration.
