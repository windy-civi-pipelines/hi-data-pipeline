NO. 423 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII H.D. 2 C.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE EARLY LEARNING BOARD. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Section 302L-1.6, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by amending subsection (b) to read as follows: "(b)  The board shall consist of the following voting members: (1)  One voting member from the county of Hawaii; (2)  One voting member from the county of Maui; (3)  One voting member from the county of Kauai; (4)  Three voting members from the city and county of Honolulu; and (5)  Three at-large voting members[.]; provided that one voting member shall have experience as a local provider of early childhood education and development services. The superintendent of education, director of human services, director of health, [and] president of the [University] university of Hawaii, and director of the Hawaii head start state collaboration office shall serve as ex officio, nonvoting members of the board. The board shall invite the chief executive officer of Kamehameha Schools [and], the executive director of the Hawaii Association of Independent Schools, and president of the Head Start Association of Hawaii, or their designees, to serve as ex officio, [non-voting] nonvoting members of the board." SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Report Title: Early Learning Board; Composition; Membership; Hawaii Head Start State Collaboration Office; Head Start Association of Hawaii Description: Requires one voting member of the Early Learning Board to have experience as a local provider of early childhood education and development services.  Adds the Director of the Hawaii Head Start State Collaboration Office as an ex officio, nonvoting member to the Early Learning Board.  Requires the Early Learning Board to invite the President of the Head Start Association of Hawaii, or their designee, to serve as an ex officio, nonvoting member of the Board.  (CD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent. ================================================================================ Raw Text: SB423 CD1 THE SENATE S.B.
