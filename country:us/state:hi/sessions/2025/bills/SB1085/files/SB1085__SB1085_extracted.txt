Section 1:
SB1085 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 1085 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the department of agriculture. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $ or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the preparation of an environmental impact statement for the drilling of non-potable water wells on individual parcels at Panaewa agricultural park and Panaewa farm lots. The sums appropriated shall be expended by the department of agriculture for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
