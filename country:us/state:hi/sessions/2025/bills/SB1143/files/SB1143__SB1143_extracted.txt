Section 1:
SB1143 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 1143 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO TOURISM. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Act 128, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, is amended by amending section 3 to read as follows: "SECTION 3.  Once the Hawaii tourism authority prepares and submits an updated tourism functional plan pursuant to this Act and section 226‑56, Hawaii Revised Statutes, the governor shall transmit the approved tourism functional plan to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the subsequent regular session [of 2025.]; provided that the preparation of the updated tourism plan shall be subject to the availability of funding." SECTION 2. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Tourism; HTA; Functional Plan Description: Requires annual submission of an updated tourism functional plan by the Hawaiian Tourism Authority, subject to available funding.
