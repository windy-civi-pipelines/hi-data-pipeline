A BILL FOR AN ACT
MAKING APPROPRIATIONS TO PROVIDE FOR THE EXPENSES OF THE
LEGISLATURE, THE AUDITOR, THE LEGISLATIVE REFERENCE BUREAU,

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. In accordance with section 9 of article VII of
2 the Constitution of the State of Hawaii and sections 37-91 and
3 37-93, Hawaii Revised Statutes, the legislature has determined
4 that the general fund expenditure ceiling for fiscal year
5 2024-2025 has already been exceeded by $8,551,795, or 1.0 per
6 cent. The appropriations contained in sections 2, 3, and 13 of
7 this Act will cause the state general fund expenditure ceiling
8 for fiscal year 2024-2025 to be further exceeded by $30,334,159,
9 or 1.0 per cent. This declaration takes into account the
10 additional general fund appropriations authorized for fiscal
11 year 2024-2025 in this Act only. The reasons for exceeding the
12 general fund expenditure ceiling are that:
13 (1) The appropriations made in this Act are necessary to
14 serve the public interest; and
15 (2) The appropriations made in this Act meet the needs
16 addressed by this Act.
