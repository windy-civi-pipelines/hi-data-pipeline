Section 1:
SB583 SD2 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 583 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 S.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO NAMING RIGHTS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 102, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§102-     Naming rights of state-owned facilities; marketing; advertising.  (a)  The naming rights for any state‑owned facility may be leased to any public or private entity. (b)  Any revenues derived from advertising or marketing in or on any state-owned facility, including revenues derived under subsection (a), shall be deposited into the appropriate special fund of the state agency that owns the facility or into the general fund if no appropriate special fund exists." SECTION 2. New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2050. Report Title: Naming Rights; State-Owned Facilities; Concessions; Advertising; Marketing Description: Allows the naming rights of any state-owned facility to be leased to any public or private entity.  Requires any revenues derived from advertising or marketing in or on any state-owned facility to be deposited into the appropriate special fund of the state agency that owns the facility or into the general fund if no appropriate special fund exists.  Effective 7/1/2050.  (SD2) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
