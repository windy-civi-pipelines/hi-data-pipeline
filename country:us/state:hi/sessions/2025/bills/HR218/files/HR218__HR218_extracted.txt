HR218 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 218 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION informing the senate and governor that the house of representatives is ready to adjourn sine die. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that the House of Representatives stands ready to adjourn Sine Die; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the President of the Senate and Governor.
